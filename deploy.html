Manual Deployment Steps (if automatic deployment fails)

  1. Log in to your Supabase Dashboard
  2. Navigate to Storage â†’ Buckets
  3. Click "Create Bucket" and name it mindaid-app
  4. Make sure to check "Public Bucket" option
  5. Click on the newly created bucket
  6. Click "Upload File" and upload all the following files:
    • index.html
    • actions.html
    • journal.html
    • scripts.js
    • config.js
  7. After uploading, access your app at:
    https://[your-project-url].supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/mindaid-app/index.html
